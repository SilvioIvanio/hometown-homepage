Visit Luanda

Enjoy the vibrant culture and stunning coastline of Angola's capital city.

Top three activities to do in Luanda

Experience the historic Fortress of São Miguel

Discover centuries of history and enjoy panoramic views of the city and bay from this iconic fortress.

Relax at Mussulo Island

Take a boat trip to this beautiful island paradise, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying fresh seafood.

Explore the Museu da Moeda (Currency Museum)

Discover Angola's fascinating economic history through engaging exhibits on currency, located beneath the beautiful waterfront promenade.
